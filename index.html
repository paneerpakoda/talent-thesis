The Work · StakesMeridian

The highest-stakes canvas in the country.

Clarity beats clever
When the screen holds someone's salary and savings, the boldest move is making it unmistakable.
Error states matter most
The screen that quietly stops a wrong transfer is the work no one thanks you for — and the work that counts.
Trust is the metric
Not time-on-app or streaks. Did they feel safe moving their money — and were they right to?
A confusing flow here isn't a bounce — it's real harm. That raises the bar on every decision you make.
